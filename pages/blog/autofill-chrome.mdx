# How to Use Autofill in Chrome: Complete Guide to Autofill Chrome Extension

## Introduction: What is Autofill in Chrome?

**Autofill in Chrome** is a built-in feature that helps users fill out forms automatically by storing and filling in personal information such as names, addresses, and payment details. While this native autofill feature is useful for basic forms, it has its limitations. To unlock more advanced capabilities, such as autofilling complex web forms or Google Forms, you can turn to **Chrome extensions for autofill**.

In this guide, we’ll explore how to use **autofill chrome** and take your form-filling experience to the next level with an **autofill Chrome extension**—specifically, the **AutoFormAI** extension. Whether you're completing a survey, signing up for a service, or checking out online, **autofill in Chrome** can save you a lot of time. By storing your details securely in your browser, Chrome can automatically fill out forms when you revisit sites.
