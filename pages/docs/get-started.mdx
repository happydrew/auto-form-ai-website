### 4. Installing AutoFormAI
- Go to the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/autoformai/lfikjlhmofcmjbimgjjnafdeljkfhpej/) and install AutoFormAI.
- Once installed, the AutoFormAI icon will appear on the right side of any webpage‚Äîyour control panel is always ready.

### 5. Logging in
- Click the icon to log in.
- You can use your Google account or register with your email. The first login may take a moment, but it's worth it!

### 6. Creating an Information Library
- After logging in, you'll land on the AutoFormAI options page. This is where you can manage libraries, check credits, and more.

### 7. Creating a Library from Email Content
- Click "Create Library."
- Fill in the name and description, and paste the information into the content field.
- Hit "Create" to save your library. You can create libraries from any text source‚Äînot just emails! üìù

### 8. Autofilling the Form
- Returning to the form page, you'll see that the AutoFormAI control panel on the right is already logged in.
- In the AutoFormAI panel, select your newly created library from the dropdown menu.
- Click "Autofill Now" and watch as the form fills automatically. Your form is complete in seconds! üôå‚ú®
